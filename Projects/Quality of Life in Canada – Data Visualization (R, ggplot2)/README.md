## Overview
Analyzed key Quality of Life indicators in Canada and presented insights through
clear visualizations and an infographic.

## Why this project
To explore how social, economic, environmental, and governance indicators change
over time and communicate insights in a way that is easy for non-technical audiences
to understand.

## Data
- Statistics Canada (Quality of Life Framework)
- Indicators across five dimensions:
  - Prosperity
  - Health
  - Environment
  - Society
  - Governance

## Approach
- Cleaned and transformed public datasets using R
- Created visualizations for each indicator
- Combined results into a single infographic to highlight trends and patterns

## Indicators Analyzed
- Life expectancy at birth
- Median after-tax income
- Greenhouse gas emissions
- Confidence in federal parliament
- Sense of belonging to local community

## Key Insights
- Strong performance in prosperity, health, and social belonging
- Environmental sustainability and trust in governance show weaker trends
- Visual storytelling makes complex public data easier to interpret

## Tools
R, tidyverse, ggplot2, Statistics Canada datasets
